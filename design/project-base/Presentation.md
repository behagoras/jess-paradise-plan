## What are we building

- A travel booking app for finding trips based on budget and preferences for those who like to travel but don't particularly have a destination in mind focussing in cost efficiency

## Users want/need

**Price Sensitivity**: People are very concerned with pricing and budgeting for trips
**Usability**: People want easy intueitivity tools to save time and concern
**Options**: People love to travel, however choosing where is a hassle due to budget restictions and lack of ideas
**Convenience**: Individuals want easy fast access to information, options and availability
